Who We Are

AZ Swamp Cooler Repair (azswampcoolerrepair.com) is a local lead-generation and service-request website for swamp cooler and evaporative cooler repair in the Phoenix East Valley.

Information We Collect

When you call, email, or submit a quote request, we may collect:

  • Name, phone number, and email address
  • City, service address area, and details about your cooler or repair need
  • Preferred timing or urgency of service

How We Use Information

We use this information to:

  • Respond to your service request
  • Route your call or form submission to an independent local service provider who can follow up about your repair request

By submitting a form or calling the number on this site, you understand that your contact information and service request details may be shared with an independent local service provider for follow-up. That provider is responsible for its own pricing, availability, workmanship, licensing, insurance, and warranty terms.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. Google may collect device and usage information according to its own policies.

Data Retention

We retain lead and website data as long as needed for business operations, lead follow-up, recordkeeping, and dispute resolution, unless a longer period is required by law.

Your Choices

You may request access to or correction of personal information you submitted by contacting us at info@azswampcoolerrepair.com. You are not required to submit a form to browse the site.

Children

Changes
