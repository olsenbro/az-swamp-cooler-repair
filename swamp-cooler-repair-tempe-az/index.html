Common Swamp Cooler Issues in Tempe

Tempe's older housing stock means local providers often encounter vintage cooler models and non-standard installations. Properties near ASU and the urban core sometimes have coolers mounted in unusual locations or wired in ways that reflect decades of DIY maintenance by successive tenants or owners. Mentioning the age and location of the unit can help the provider who follows up understand the likely service needs.

  • Unit running but blowing warm or room-temperature air
  • Cooler not turning on at all
  • Grinding, squealing, or rattling sounds
  • Water leaking or pooling around the unit
  • Pads visibly brown, crusted, or moldy
  • Reduced airflow even on high speed
  • Motor running but fan not spinning
